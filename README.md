# Flex AI

An AI-powered strength and recovery coach designed to help you train smarter, not harder.

## Features

- **Personalized Workouts & Recovery Plans**: Tell the AI what you want to achieve or recover from, and it instantly generates a custom plan tailored to your needs.
- **Smart Intensity Modes**: Choose between 'Train' for building your legacy and 'Recover' for getting back in the game. Select from Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced intensity levels.
- **Save Your Favorites**: Instantly find and replay your top workouts or recovery routines with a single tap.
- **Spotify Integration**: Vibe out during your sessions with integrated Spotify music links customized for upbeat training or calm recovery.
- **Instant Exercise Demos**: Tap any exercise name to instantly watch a YouTube demonstration.
- **XP & Levels (Dumbbells)**: Earn "Dumbbells" (XP) for completing workouts. Level up and track your progress through a weekly progress bar and a 28-day consistency heatmap.
- **Smart Rest Timers**: Built-in rest timers between sets keep you on track.
- **Google Sign-In & Firebase Sync**: Secure authentication to keep your stats and favorites synced.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Flutter
- **AI Integration**: Google Generative AI
- **Authentication**: Firebase Authentication & Google Sign-In
- **Local Storage**: SharedPreferences
